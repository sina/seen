<section class="mb-8">Team Camaraderie is the spirit of trust, friendship and loyalty present in well
functioning teams. A common framework for describing and measuring this is called the 5C's of teamwork<FootnoteLink index={1} />.</section>

<section>
  <ul class="list-inside">
    <li class="mb-8 ">
      <ul class="list-inside">
        <li class="font-bold">Communication</li>
        <li>Clear and open communication is essential to maintaining trust and building confidence among
        the team. Goals and risks are defined clearly and the rate of success becomes safely predictable.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li class="mb-8">
      <ul class="list-inside">
        <li class="font-bold">Commitment</li>
        <li>A shared goal amongst teammates is foundational to demonstrate commitment. Working together
        can improve the perfomance of the entire team allowing accomplishments very difficult to achieve
        by even siloed masters.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li class="mb-8">
      <ul class="list-inside">
        <li class="font-bold">Confidence</li>
        <li>Having faith in your fellow teammates allows suggestions, concerns and creativity to flow
        more readily and often.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li class="mb-8">
      <ul class="list-inside">
        <li class="font-bold">Contribution</li>
        <li>Teammates bring skills that complement others and improve overall quality and success.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li class="mb-8">
      <ul class="list-inside">
        <li class="font-bold">Coordination</li>
        <li>The team operates as a single unit with confidence in each moving part and with a vested
        interest in the success of both the team and fellow teammates.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ul>
</section>

#### How to build authentic team spirit
<section class="mb-8">
  <ul class="list-inside">
    <li class="font-bold">Build rapport beyond work life</li>
    <li class="list-disc">Take teams out for lunch or coffee regularly and grow team bonds with humor and banter.</li>
    <li class="list-disc">Create team brown-bag<FootnoteLink index={2} /> lunches that allow teammates to present tech topics that are not necessarily related to work.</li>
    <li class="list-disc">Get to know your teammate's hobbies, likes and life.</li>
    <li class="list-disc">Take 1:1s out of the office with walks covering short and long term career development.</li>
    
    <li class="font-bold mt-4">Promote and reward team excellence</li>
    <li class="list-disc">Showcase technical accomplishments internally and to the user community through meetup presentations.</li>
    <li class="list-disc">Utilize events to improve/demonstrate public speaking, writing and leadership and other career development goals.<FootnoteLink index={3} /></li>
    <li class="list-disc">When the team hits goals or has achievements, call it out and celebrate.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
