<p class="mb-8">This project was a virtual tour of the UCLA campus and the Anderson School in a
  webinar format, led by current students. Designed to provide prospective
  students with an engaging and informative experience of campus life and
  academic programs using video Q&A.</p>

<p>Architected and launched online university tour startup using Grails,
  Java services and Zoom webinar services. Leveraged AWS S3 storage,
  ELB load balancers, SES email service and EC2 compute cloud services for nimble scalability
  and easy distribution of email invitations and video artifacts to attendees.</p>
