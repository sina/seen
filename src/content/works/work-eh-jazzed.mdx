<p class="mb-8">Jazzed was a social dating webapp backed by eHarmony's deep matching technology
  to provide a social-connection and shared-interest based suggestion
  engine for finding your other half. Jazzed was closed in September 2012.</p>

<p>Successfully led features development and front-end architectural improvements
  moving from dojo/AJAX to modular js using event-driven programming and RESTful
  data binding, SOLR content matching and gamified A/B tests to foster user engagement.</p>
