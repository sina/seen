<p class="mb-8">Following a 7.6 magnitude earthquake that damaged or destroyed
  3/4 of a million homes, this non-profit website and foundation was created
  to raise funds, provide support and rebuild homes for a Nepalese families.</p>

<p>Setup a Wordpress CMS website that integrated with Instagram, Facebook,
Twitter and Paypal in a few hours of volunteering. The Bootstrap responsive website helped build 100 homes in the
first two weeks.</p>
